How does the comparison work?

We use an optimized diff algorithm to identify additions, deletions, and modifications. You can compare text at the word level, character level, or line level depending on your needs.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All text comparison happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your machine and is never sent to our servers.
